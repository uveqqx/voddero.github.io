Personal automation tool that collects top clips from live streaming platforms and publishes them to short-form video channels automatically.

## What it does

- 📥 **Clip Collection** — Fetches trending clips from Twitch and Kick via their official APIs
- ✂️ **Auto Formatting** — Reformats clips to vertical 9:16 and adds captions for each platform
- 📤 **Multi-Platform Upload** — Publishes to TikTok, Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts automatically
- 🔒 **Personal Use Only** — This tool operates on a single account and is not distributed publicly

## Privacy Policy

Personal automation tool operated by a single individual for personal use. This application is not publicly available and does not collect, store, or share any personal data from third parties.

The tool accesses the TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube APIs solely to publish content to accounts owned and operated by the developer. No user data, browsing data, or personally identifiable information is collected at any point.

Content published through this tool consists of gaming clips sourced from Twitch and Kick via their official APIs, with proper attribution to the original creators. All API usage complies with the respective platforms' Terms of Service and Developer Policies.
