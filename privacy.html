PRIVACY POLICY — VODdero Last updated: June 21, 2026 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT VODdero collects only the information necessary to operate the service: - TikTok account access tokens (OAuth 2.0), used solely to publish content on your behalf. - Basic TikTok profile information (username, profile picture) for account identification. - Video files and metadata you submit for publishing. 2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION - To authenticate and publish content to connected TikTok accounts. - To display connected account information within the VODdero dashboard. - We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. 3. DATA RETENTION Access tokens are stored securely and deleted upon account disconnection. We do not retain video content after successful publishing. 4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES VODdero integrates with the TikTok API. By connecting your TikTok account, you also agree to TikTok's Privacy Policy (tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy). 5. SECURITY We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. 6. YOUR RIGHTS You may disconnect your TikTok account at any time from the VODdero dashboard or directly from your TikTok account settings. 7.
